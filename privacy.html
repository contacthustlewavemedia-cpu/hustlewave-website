Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share with us. Short, clear, no tricks.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Section 01

Who This Applies To

This policy applies to anyone who visits hustlewave.com, messages HustleWave through Instagram or other channels, or becomes a client of HustleWave.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We only collect information we actually need to do the work. Depending on how you interact with us, that may include:

  • Business name, contact name, phone number, and email
  • Service list, service area, and business details you share
  • Images, logos, and written content you send us for the website
  • Messages sent through Instagram, email, or text
  • Basic, anonymous traffic information such as pages viewed and general location (via standard web analytics, if used)

We do not sell your information. Ever.

Section 03

How We Use It

Information you share is used strictly to:

  • Build, finalize, and launch your website
  • Host and manage the website under the monthly care plan
  • Respond to questions, support requests, or change requests
  • Send invoices, receipts, and service-related notices
Section 04

Third-Party Services

HustleWave may use third-party tools and platforms to deliver the service, such as hosting providers, domain registrars, payment processors, email tools, and analytics. These providers have their own privacy practices and we encourage you to review them.

HustleWave is not responsible for outages, limitations, or policy changes caused by third-party services.

Section 05

Cookies & Analytics

This website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Section 06

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we do not store sensitive financial details on our own systems — payments are handled by established third-party processors.

Section 07

Your Rights

You can ask us to:

  • Confirm what information we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete information we no longer need to retain

Just message us — we'll take care of it in a reasonable timeframe.

Section 08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.

Section 09

Contact

Questions about privacy? Message us on Instagram at @official_hustlewave.
